### 🎨 About Me

I'm a UI/UX Designer based in Indonesia, currently working on **Tiketux** — a multi-operator bus & shuttle booking platform — handling everything from booking funnel audits to new feature design. I also take on contract design work for transportation and reservation systems.

What sets my workflow apart: I don't just design screens, I write full PRDs, map user flows, and occasionally **build the things I design** — including a working Discord bot I coded myself to prototype an interaction flow before handing it off as a case study.

- 🔭 Currently designing booking flows & exploring membership/loyalty features for Tiketux
- 🌱 Learning Python to bridge the gap between design intent and technical feasibility
- 💡 Side project: **Talangin** — a Discord bot solving real office group-order payment headaches
- 📚 Studying Information Systems (S1, PJJ) at Universitas Siber Asia
